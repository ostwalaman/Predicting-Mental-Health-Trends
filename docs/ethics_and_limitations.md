# Ethics and Limitations

NeuroNexus analyzes aggregate patterns in public Reddit text for educational and research purposes. It is not a diagnostic system, clinical screening tool, or replacement for professional mental-health assessment.

## Data Ethics

- Reddit posts can contain sensitive personal experiences even when public.
- Raw post text, author-like handles, and personally sensitive examples should not be exposed in public notebook outputs.
- Analysis should be reported at an aggregate level whenever possible.

## Model Limitations

- Reddit communities are not representative of the broader population.
- Labels are weakly supervised and partially derived from subreddit or topic signals.
- The severity score is experimental and based on keyword-derived signals, intensifiers, qualifiers, and medication mentions.
- Severity outputs require stronger labeled data and clinical validation before any real-world use.

## Responsible Use

Do not use this project to diagnose individuals, make clinical decisions, or infer a person's mental-health status. The appropriate use case is portfolio-level demonstration of NLP, data cleaning, model evaluation, and ethical reflection.
